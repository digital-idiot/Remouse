12 /**
13  * Extends the <code>AsyncTask</code> class of the Android API.

15  * <p>
16  * It performs the following operations :
17  * <ul>
18  * <li>
19  * Displaying a <code>ProgressDialog</code>.
20  * </li>
21  * <li>
22  * Constructing a {@link project.android.security.EKEProvider} object

24  * </li>
25  * <li>
26  * Starting a {@link ClientIOThread}.
27  * </li>
28  * </ul>
29  * <code>AsyncTask</code> enables these operations to performed asynchronously
30  * by defining them in the {@link #onPreExecute()},
31  * {@link #doInBackground(ServerInfo...)} and {@link #onPostExecute(EKEProvider)}
32  * methods respectively. The starting of {@link ClientIOThread} waits for the
33  * construction of the {@link project.android.security.EKEProvider} object.
34  * A <code>ProgressDialog</code> is displayed for this duration.
35  * </p>
36  *
37  * @see project.android.ConnectionFragment
38  * @see project.android.net.ClientIOThread
39  * @see project.android.net.ServerInfo
40  * @see project.android.security.EKEProvider
