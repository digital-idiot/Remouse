Detailed Description

Class to represent a 4x4 matrix (float).

Internal the matrix is structured as :
     

       [ x0 , y0 , z0 , w0 ]
       [ x1 , y1 , z1 , w1 ]
       [ x2 , y2 , z2 , w2 ]
       [ x3 , y3 , z3 , w3 ]
